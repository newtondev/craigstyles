Biography

A South African DJ from Cape Town that has been DJ'ing since early 2000. Main venues DJ'd at were Stones and Cubana. Now a resident of the Netherlands and rekindling his love for music by making mixes.

DJ Craig Styles is a year 2000 Graduate of the Technics DMC (DJ Mix Club) under the wing of Jayson "The Fool" Faber.

Having various residencies over the past years, he really enjoyed his residency at Stones in Long Street and Table View.

DJ Craig Styles enjoys any type of music that gets people moving, from House to Hip Hop, whether it is being booked to play weddings, birthday parties, corporate functions or bigger club events.
